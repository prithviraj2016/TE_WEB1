  • Basics

  • How easy is it to sign up for Tournament Edition?

    You can simply sign up for Tournament Edition with your Facebook, Email and Twitch.

  • Tournaments

  • How do I Register for a tournament?

    Click on tournament you would like to sign up for. An option below the tournament information appears on the right side and click on Register. Now just wait for Tournament Organizer to accept it. That's it!

  • How do I create a tournament?

    Login to TE. Click on Tournaments on the navigation bar. Click on Create New. Fill in information for your tournament such as Name, Start and End date, Game Type, Game name etc. Then Click Create and that is it!

  • How do I start a tournament?

    Once your tournament is set with all the information simply click on the Start. Your tournament is ready to go!

  • How do I add/invite players to my tournaments?

    After your tournament is created click on Manage Players in the bottom right hand side. You have 3 ways of adding people to a tournament. Invite- Allows TO's to type in a players that have a TE account and send an invite notification. Bulk- Allows for TO's to allow anyone even without an account to be added and as well as a copy & paste style format but players profiles are not saved. Team- Allows for teams or individual players of a team to be invited.

  • What does Generate Pools mean?

    When you add players to a tournament you can select to Generate Pool. This allows TO's to create the amount of players to be in each pool. If Pool size is bigger than players added. A Bye will be in place of the amount. This would allow players to be added at a later time if need be without disrupting the Tournament.

  • Oh no I put in the wrong information for my tournament now what do I do!?

    You can edit any of your information you put in previously just by clicking on the Edit button on your tournament page as long as you have not started the tournament. If you have already started your tournament you can still edit some of the information though.

  • Events

  • How do I create an Event?

    Login to TE. Click on Events on Navigation bar. Click on Create New. Fill in information for your Event. Then Click Create and that's it.

  • Great I made my Event but how do I add my Tournaments to it?

    Once your Event has been created click on your Event that you want to add your tournament to. Then click on the Add Tournament button. The next screen should show you a list of all your tournaments. Simply click on the tournament that you want to add and that's it!

  • Profile

  • How do I edit any of my Profile/Account setting?

    Click on "My Profile" at the top of the Navigation bar. Then click "Edit Profile". There will be various options for you to change. Such as Display Name, Email, Location, User Picture, etc.

  • Miscellaneous

  • What is the difference between Tournaments, Events and Seasons?

    Tournaments is where players actually get ranked and is individual. Events is used for a hub of tournaments. Seasons is a hub of Events. Think of Seasons as the Olympics then the Events are the track events and the Tournaments are the individual Men's 400m.

  • How do I add admins to my tournaments/events/seasons?

    Go to your tournament info page. Then click on Staff next to your Edit button add staff member from your follower list and that's it! To add an admin to your tournament the user must have a TE account and must be following you.

  • I accidently clicked on a Tournament to be added to my Event I did not want to include!?

    Simply go to your Add Tournament button and click on the Tournament you want to remove and that is it!

  • What is the Hype feature?

    With Hype you can promote your tournaments and events to reach people on the local or national. It is $0.99 for 1 Hype to reach 100 people locally (via front page and hype on app 100 mile radius) or $1.99 for 1 Hype to reach 100 people nationally.
